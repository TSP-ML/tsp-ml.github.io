Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions. Can't find what you're looking for? Use the contact form above.

How do I get API access?

API access is available for developers and businesses. Contact us through the form above with "API Access" as the subject, and we'll get you set up with documentation and credentials.

Is TSP(ML) free to use?

Our web demo is completely free with a limit of 10 locations. For larger problems or commercial use, we offer various plans including enterprise solutions.

Can you help with custom implementations?

Yes! We offer consulting services for custom optimization problems and can help integrate TSP(ML) into your existing systems. Contact us for a consultation.

Do you offer technical support?

We provide technical support for all our users. Free users get community support, while premium and enterprise customers receive priority support.

How can I contribute to the project?

We welcome contributions! Check out our GitHub repository for open issues, or reach out to discuss new features and improvements you'd like to work on.

Are you hiring?

We're always interested in connecting with talented developers and researchers. Send us your resume and let us know how you'd like to contribute to making optimization accessible.
