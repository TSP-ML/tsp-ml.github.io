About TSP(ML)

Meet the passionate team behind the mission to make optimization accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

We believe that powerful optimization tools should be accessible to everyone, not just researchers and large corporations. The Travelling Salesman Problem (TSP) is one of the most fundamental optimization challenges, yet existing solutions are either too complex for everyday users or lack the sophistication needed for real-world applications.

TSP(ML) bridges this gap by combining cutting-edge machine learning algorithms with intuitive, accessible interfaces. Whether you're a delivery driver planning the most efficient route, a student learning about optimization, or a business owner trying to minimize travel costs, our platform makes advanced routing solutions available at your fingertips.

Our Story

The Problem

We noticed a significant gap in the market: while services like Google Maps excel at finding the shortest path between two points, they don't solve the more complex problem of finding the optimal route through multiple destinations. This limitation affects millions of people daily - from delivery drivers to sales representatives to tourists planning sightseeing routes.

The Vision

We envisioned a world where advanced optimization algorithms are as accessible as sending a text message. Why should powerful routing optimization be limited to large logistics companies with dedicated IT teams? Everyone deserves access to tools that can save them time, money, and reduce environmental impact.

The Solution

Combining our diverse expertise in machine learning, frontend development, and mobile app design, we created TSP(ML) - a platform that makes sophisticated optimization algorithms accessible through beautiful, intuitive interfaces. Our approach democratizes technology that was previously available only to large corporations.

The Impact

Today, TSP(ML) helps thousands of users optimize their routes, saving time and reducing carbon footprints. But we're just getting started. Our vision extends beyond routing to making all kinds of optimization problems accessible, fostering a world where intelligent algorithms serve everyone, not just the privileged few.

Our Values

Accessibility First

We believe technology should be usable by everyone, regardless of their technical background or abilities. Every feature we build is designed with accessibility and inclusivity in mind.

Transparency

We're committed to open-source development and transparent algorithms. Users should understand how their data is processed and how our algorithms make decisions.

Sustainability

By optimizing routes and reducing unnecessary travel, we contribute to environmental sustainability. Every efficient route is a step toward a greener future.

Community-Driven

Our development is guided by user feedback and community contributions. We build for real people solving real problems, not abstract theoretical challenges.
